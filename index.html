How many kanji do you know?

This is a tool designed to evaluate your current level of knowledge of kanji (Chinese characters) used in Japanese. You will be presented characters and asked to select the correct pronunciation and meaning in English in a multiple choice format. At the end of the test, you will be given an estimate of the approximate amount of kanji you know.

The goal of the test is to give you an idea of how many kanji you know in order to help you evaluate your current level and guide your study goals. Test Your Kanji Knowledge

The goal of this test is to estimate the amount of kanji you know. You will be presented with a kanji prompt, and be asked to select the correct reading and English definition from four choices. If you do not know the answer, you can simply click the "I don't know" button. Submitting "I don't know" has the exact same effect as submitting an incorrect answer. You are encouraged, however, to not guess if you are unsure of the correct answer. Guessing or using process of elimination when you are unsure of the answer will result in a more inaccurate calculation of your knowledge.

The test should take 5-10 minutes. You must complete the entire test to see your results.

Results given are an estimation. If you would like a more accurate calculation of your knowledge, try taking the test multiple times and averaging your results.

Good luck!

About

This tool was developed with the purpose of assisting you in estimating your proficiency in Kanji (Chinese characters used in Japanese). It utilizes a curated list of the 6,355 most frequently used Kanji. The test prompts have been randomized, ensuring a distinct experience each time you undertake the assessment. While the application's algorithm has been carefully designed to accurately evaluate your knowledge, the inherent variability of the randomized test prompts may result in score fluctuations between evaluations. I recommend taking the test multiple times and averaging the results to obtain a more precise assessment of your proficiency.
